Our Donate Plan

There are many ways you can help make a difference for children and girls. For example, you can make a one-time or monthly donation, buy a Gift of Hope, sponsor a child or make a lasting impact with a legacy gift.

Plan for Children Food

Donate today to help get life-saving food to the children and families who need it the most. Our work is 100% voluntarily funded and every donation makes a difference. It only takes a minute to donate, but the impact can last a lifetime. 

Plan for Children Fund

When you make a monthly contribution to the Plan for Children Fund, you help support high-priority and long-term projects that advance children’s rights and equality for girls. 

Plan for Family Fund
